Who is Nate Nuval?

"She told me, boy you want your cake and eat it too. I said it's cake, thats what your supposed to do" -J.Cole

I am a first generation Filipino American born in Chicago, and raised in Northern VA. I attended Heritage High school and spent my weekends and breaks working at my family's restaurant, Windy City Red Hots. After graduating high school in 2012 with a 2.5 GPA, I went to Radford University for my freshman year and half of my sophomore year. At this point I considered myself a C average student; my grades reflected this sentiment. I took the Spring semester off and worked as a manager at Windy City Red Hots. I used this time to do some soul searching (the food service industry will do that to you). I was not satisfied with "C average", I needed to go back to college, and I needed to get myself organized and motivated. I took courses at Northern Virginia Community College (NVCC) for the Summer, Fall, and Spring semesters. I left NVCC with a 3.78 GPA and even took 1st place in the Best Speaker Competition. I transferred to the University of Mary Washington (UMW) in 2015 and continued to pursue a degree in Computer Science. During that first year I joined the UMW Boxing Club, hosted several humanitarian mapping events, and made the President’s List with my 4.0 GPA. Who is Nate Nuval? A couple of years ago I was a smart kid who was too lazy and unmotivated to try hard. Today, I’m not the smartest guy in the room who effortlessly succeeds and never struggles with assignments. I am the guy who puts in extra time and effort when faced with a difficult task, who isn’t afraid to ask questions, and who enjoys learning new things.
